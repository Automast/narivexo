Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our electrical services.

What areas do you serve?
We provide electrical services throughout our service area. Call us to confirm we serve your location and to schedule an appointment for your electrical needs.
Are your electricians licensed and insured?
Yes, all electricians working on your project are fully licensed and insured. We maintain all necessary certifications and insurance coverage to protect you and your property.
Do you handle emergency electrical repairs?
Yes, we understand that electrical emergencies can happen at any time. Contact us immediately if you're experiencing an electrical emergency such as power outages, sparking outlets, or burning smells from electrical components.
How long does typical electrical work take?
The timeline varies depending on the scope of work. Simple repairs may take just a few hours, while larger projects like panel upgrades or whole-home rewiring can take several days. We'll provide a clear timeline when we assess your project.
What should I do if I smell burning from an outlet?
If you smell burning from an outlet or electrical panel, turn off power to that circuit immediately and call us right away. This could indicate a serious electrical problem that requires immediate professional attention.
Do you provide warranties on your work?
We stand behind the quality of our work and materials. We'll discuss specific warranty details for your project when we provide your service information.
